Turn on your device

Use the Home button to turn your watch on or off.

  • Press and hold the Home button.
    • To turn the watch off, swipe down from the top of the watch screen to open the Quick panel, and tap > Turn off.
TIP To restart the watch, press and hold the Home and Back buttons for more than seven seconds.
