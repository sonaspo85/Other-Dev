Accessibility

There are accessibility settings for people who need help seeing, hearing, or otherwise operating their device. Accessibility services are special features that make using the device easier for everyone.

Learn more: accessibility@sea.samsung.com

TalkBack

Receive spoken feedback when using your device, such as what you touch, select, or activate.

  1. From Settings, tap Accessibility > TalkBack.
  2. Tap to enable the feature.
    • To configure TalkBack, tap Settings.
