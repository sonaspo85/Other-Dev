Water lock mode

You can use Water lock mode to exercise in the water while wearing your watch. While in Water lock mode, touchscreen, wake-up gestures, and watch always on will be disabled.

  • From the Quick settings panel, tap to put the watch in Water lock mode.
TIP You can also enable Water lock mode from the Setting menu. From
Settings, tap Advanced features > Water lock. Tap to enable.

Exit water lock mode

  1. Press and hold the Home button for two seconds to turn off the mode. The watch will make sounds to eject water from the speaker.
  2. Press the Home button to stop the sound.
  3. Shake the watch to remove any water from the pressure sensor.
