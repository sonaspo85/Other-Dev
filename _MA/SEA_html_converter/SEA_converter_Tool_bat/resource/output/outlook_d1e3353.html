Outlook

Open new emails on your watch.

  1. From Apps, tap Outlook.
  2. Tap a message to review its content.

Open calendar events

Open your Outlook calendar events on your watch.

  • From Outlook, tap an event to open the content.

Notification settings

Customize Microsoft Outlook

  • From Outlook, tap Notifications and tap an option to customize:
    • Mail: Choose to receive notifications in the same way as your smartphone, or set a vibration option.
    • Calendar: Choose to receive notifications in the same way as your smartphone, or set a vibration option.
