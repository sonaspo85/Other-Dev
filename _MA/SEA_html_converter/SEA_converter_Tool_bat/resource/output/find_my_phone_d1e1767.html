Find My Phone

If you misplace your smartphone, your watch can help you find it.

  1. From Apps, tap Find My Phone.
  2. Tap Start.
    • The smartphone vibrates and the screen turns on.
  3. Drag Dismiss on your smartphone or tap Stop on the watch.

Find your Watch

You can also locate your watch by making it light up and make sounds from the Galaxy Wearable app.

  1. From Galaxy Wearable, tap Find My Watch.
  2. Tap Start.
  3. Tap Stop on the smartphone or drag on the watch.
