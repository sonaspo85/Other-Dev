Music

Play music from your watch.

  1. From Apps, tap Music.
  2. Tap an album, playlist, or track to begin playing.

Import music

Import music saved on your smartphone to your watch.

  1. From the smartphone, tap Galaxy Wearable, tap Watch settings.
  2. Tap Manage content > Tracks on watch > Add .
  3. Select files and tap Add to watch.

To sync recently added music on your smartphone with your watch:

  1. From the smartphone, tap Galaxy Wearable, tap Watch settings.
  2. Tap Manage content > Auto sync, music syncs while the watch’s battery power is at least fifteen percent.
