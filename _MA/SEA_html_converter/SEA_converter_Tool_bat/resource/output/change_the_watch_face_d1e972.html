Change the watch face

The first screen when you turn on the Galaxy Watch6 and Galaxy Watch6 Classic is the watch face. You can choose a watch face with different colors, styles, and information displayed.

From your smartphone:

  1. From the smartphone, tap Galaxy Wearable > Watch faces.
  2. Tap a watch face to select it.

From your watch:

  1. Touch and hold the watch face.
  2. Swipe left and tap Edit on phone or swipe right and tap Add watch face.
  3. Tap Customize to select a background color and items to display on the watch face.
