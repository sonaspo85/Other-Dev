Activation

The Galaxy Watch6 and Galaxy Watch6 Classic are 4G LTE-enabled. Get texts, alerts, make and receive calls, even when you leave your smartphone behind.

To manage your watch's mobile plan:

  • From the smartphone, tap Galaxy wearable, tap Watch settings >
    Mobile plans    . Consult your service provider for more information about activation.
